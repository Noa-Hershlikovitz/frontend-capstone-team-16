# frontend-capstone-team-16
This project is built by Team 16


## Description
Build Me is a website that anyone can use to create their own CV.  
In the website you can see samples of CV's that you can create in the website and which companies their workers used our website.  
You can read about our company and get some FREE tips from us.  
You can see Reviews from our customers and leave your review after using our website  
The main attraction is "CV-Creator" page when you can really create your own CV and see in real-time how your CV will look and in the end you can print/save the CV you created for future use.

## How to use Our website
1. Enter our website link
2. If you want to see CV templates click the arrows near the carrousel
3. You can scroll down and click on "Click to create" button/ Click on CV-CREATOR tab to create your own CV
4. Click on ABOUT US tab to enter the page about us
5. In about us page you can read more about us if you click on "Quick Resume Tips" you can see Free tips how to write your own CV
6. Clock on REVIEWS to enter reviews page
7. In reviews page you will see reviews that our customers wrote about the use of the website (If you hover the heart symbol you will have a nice Surprise üòâ)
8. Scroll down and you can add your own review
9. In cv creator page you create your own CV
10. You can choose if you want to work in dark/light mode
11. First, you pick the color of your CV
12. After that enter your information and you will see them appear on the live preview page
13. Pressing on "Print/Save PDF" and you have to option to print/save your own CV as PDF file
14. Pressing on Reset button and all the information that you wrote will delete
